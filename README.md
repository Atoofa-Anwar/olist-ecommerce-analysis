##Olist E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

#Project Overview
This project analyzes **100,000+ real orders** from Olist — 
Brazil's largest e-commerce marketplace.
The goal was to uncover actionable business insights on sales trends, 
delivery performance, customer behavior, and payment preferences
using Python and Power BI.

---

## Business Questions Answered
Question
1. Which month had the highest number of orders? 
2. Which product category is most popular?
3. What payment method do customers prefer? 
4. How is the overall delivery performance?
 5.  What is the order status distribution? 



## Key Insights
 **November 2017** was the peak month — driven by **Black Friday** sales
   **Bed & Bath** is the most popular category with **11,823 orders**
  **73.7% customers** pay by **Credit Card**
   Average delivery time is **12 days** — room for improvement
   **97% orders** are successfully delivered
  One order took **209 days** to deliver — identified as outlier
